Opening balance / 15 Apr 2024

FY 2024 opens on 15 April 2024: Bill graduates, turns 24, and receives 1,000 euro as a gift. No job, no savings history, no debt. This is the baseline.

April and May are months without income. Bill spends approximately 200 euro per month while searching for work, covering transport, phone, and social expenses. By the end of May he has 600 euro remaining.

In June 2024 he starts his first job at 900 euro net per month. He still lives with his parents, so his fixed costs are minimal. The monthly surplus is 495 euro. In July he buys a smartphone for 400, capitalized as a fixed asset. By December, after actual Christmas spending of 420 against a 300 plan, his cash reaches 2,970 euro. With the smartphone at net book value 345, net worth closes at 3,315.
