The gap

He earns 56% more than he did a year ago. He saves 63% less per month.

The difference is not an error. It is structural cost: rent, food, utilities, transport. These did not exist when he lived with his parents. His income statement changed in September 2025 and the surplus never recovered.

Accounting has a word for everything that feels confusing in personal finance. This system applies that vocabulary to Bill's real numbers, one decision at a time, starting from 1,000 euro.

The system

Object 01 / EP

Each episode is a ledger entry

One decision by Bill. One accounting concept. Numbers that update the system state. Every EP is an atomic record: self-contained, traceable, part of the sequence.

Object 02 / FY

Each fiscal year is a season

FY2024 opened the books. FY2025 tested going concern. FY2026 is the restatement: three years of decisions, one ledger. Each FY page is a financial statement, not a blog archive.

Object 03 / Tamagotchi

The Tamagotchi is his dashboard

Every EP includes an interactive financial dashboard: Bill's ledger, his four vitals, and the accounting insight behind the decision. It is not a widget. It is a management dashboard abstraction of his balance sheet.
