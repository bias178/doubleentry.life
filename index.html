Double Entry Life applies the logic of double entry to money, time, energy, attention and decisions. To read more clearly what builds value and what quietly consumes it over time.

A different lens on personal balance

Budget and savings are useful, but they do not describe the full picture of a personal balance sheet. Here, income, expenses, time, energy, skills, habits and relationships are all treated as resources and commitments that shape your net worth over time.

The Double Entry Life method

A simple framework to read how resources, choices and impacts accumulate into a personal balance over time.

Resources

Money, time, energy, skills, relationships, attention and opportunities available to you.

→

Choices

How you allocate, commit or protect those resources in daily and strategic decisions.

→

Impacts

Effects on your income, expenses, risk, options and well being, both now and later.

→

Balance over time

The evolution of your personal net worth, visible and invisible, across months and years.

The perspective behind Double Entry Life

Double Entry Life is an editorial project born at the intersection of finance experience and everyday questions about value, priorities and personal sustainability.

The voice behind the project comes from years spent in accounting, reporting and systems, translated into a language that stays rigorous without becoming technical for its own sake.
