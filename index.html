Bill's management dashboard / FY 2026

The gap

His bank account shows 450. Accounting shows 42.

The difference is not an error. It is depreciation: the monthly cost of owning a car that loses value whether or not a payment is due. Bill's bank statement records cash. It does not record costs.

Accounting has a word for everything that feels confusing in personal finance. This system applies that vocabulary to one person's real numbers, one decision at a time, across three fiscal years.

Object 01 / EP

Each episode is a ledger entry

One decision by Bill. One accounting concept. Numbers that update the system state. Every EP is an atomic record: self-contained, traceable, part of the sequence.

Object 02 / FY

Each fiscal year is a season

FY2024 opened the books. FY2025 tested going concern. FY2026 is the restatement: three years of decisions, one ledger. Each FY page is a financial statement, not a blog archive.
