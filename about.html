About Double Entry Life

Double Entry Life is an editorial project that reads personal life through the logic of balance sheets. It brings clarity to decisions, resources and long term sustainability.

The perspective behind the project

The project was born from years of experience in accounting, reporting and financial systems. Over time, the same logic used to read companies started to reveal patterns in personal life too.

Money is only one part of a personal balance. Time, energy, skills, relationships and attention also shape long term value. Double Entry Life gives these elements a clear language.

The goal is not to turn life into a spreadsheet. The goal is to see what is usually invisible and make better decisions with less noise.

What you will find here

Articles, models and simple tools that help you read your personal balance with clarity. No formulas, no jargon, no unrealistic productivity rules.

Who this project is for

Anyone who wants to understand how their choices accumulate over time. Anyone who wants to build a sustainable personal balance, not only a financial one.
