How to read this system

Bill started with 1,000 euro in April 2024, the day he graduated. No job, no savings, no debt. Every number since then is traceable to a specific decision recorded in the ledger.

Net worth is always derived. It is the sum of assets minus liabilities at any given EP boundary. It is never manually set.

Breaks in publishing do not mean breaks in Bill's life. Every month is accounted for, whether or not an episode was published that month.
