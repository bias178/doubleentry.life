Start where you are.

Double Entry Life is a series about one person learning to read his own financial ledger. Every article is a real-life decision explained through accounting. You can start anywhere.

01

Meet Bill

Bill is 38. He earns 2,500 net per month, pays a mortgage, drives a financed car, and wonders where his money goes. The ledger on the right shows his baseline: what he earns, what he owes, what he has. This is where every article starts.

02

Understand the format

Each article has three parts. The ledger shows Bill's numbers. The vitals track his financial health. The insight explains the accounting concept in plain language. No prior knowledge needed.

03

Pick your starting point

Start from Article 01 where Bill receives his salary and cannot quite account for where it went. Or jump to any article that feels relevant to your own life right now.
