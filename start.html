How to read this system

Bill's numbers do not change between episodes unless an episode event explicitly changes them. If EP.13 records a car purchase, every subsequent EP reflects the car loan as a liability and the depreciation as a monthly cost.

Net worth is always derived. It is the sum of assets minus liabilities at any given EP boundary. It is not manually set.

Vitals are computed from the EP context. They are not scores: they are ratios. STRESS above 60% means more than 60% of net income is committed to fixed outflows before any discretionary spending begins.
